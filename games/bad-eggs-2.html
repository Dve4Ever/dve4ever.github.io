What is Bad Eggs 2?

Bad Eggs 2 is an artillery style based multiplayer online game. In this online game, your character is a bad egg, and your mission is to destroy your enemies by controlling your egg character before they destroy you. In this game, there are lots of maps. You can try all of the maps if you are really bored and you want to have new exciting experiences. The most important about the gameplay of the game is aiming, so you should try to improve your aiming and shooting skills to get a success in this game.

How to play Bad Eggs 2

In Bad Eggs 2, for the gameplay, you should use your mouse, A, Right Arrow, Down Arrow, W, Left Arrow, space bar, D and Up Arrow keys. In order to change the power of your shoot, you should press the Down Arrow key and Up Arrow key. Besides that, for changing the angle of your shoot, you should press the Right Arrow key and Left Arrow key. You can also do all of these power and angle arrangement by using only your mouse. In simple words, you can just use the A and D keys for moving the egg left and right whereas you can jump by using the W key. Lastly, you can fire your weapon by pressing the space bar.
